Matching Adjustment Data & Analytics

A New Practice for the Solvency UK Era

The Regulatory Context

The PRA's 2024 implementation of Solvency UK represents the most structurally significant recalibration of the Matching Adjustment framework since Solvency II took effect in January 2016. The reforms — enacted through PS2/23, the updated SS3/17, and the Financial Services and Markets Act 2023 — introduce new material changes that collectively alter the operational demands placed on every MA-operating firm.

Our Response

We are launching a dedicated team of data engineers, quantitative analysts, and regulatory specialists focused exclusively on the data infrastructure and analytical products that MA-operating firms need to compete under the new framework. This is not a consulting overlay.

Our Approach

We leverage our proprietary algorithm utilising NLP and LLM capabilities to deliver a purpose-built capability, grounded in the technical realities of Solvency UK and designed to sit alongside your existing actuarial and investment functions.

The Mission

This new team exists to convert regulatory pressure into operational clarity — building the data infrastructure and analytical tooling that risk managers, chief risk officers, and boards need to satisfy the PRA, not once, but continuously, across every stage of the investment lifecycle.
