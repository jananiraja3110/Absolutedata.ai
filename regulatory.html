Matching Adjustment Data & Analytics

A new practice for the Solvency UK era purpose-built data infrastructure and analytical tooling for MA-operating firms.

Solvency UK Structural Reform

The PRA's 2024 implementation of Solvency UK enacted through PS2/23, the updated SS3/17, and the Financial Services and Markets Act 2023 represents the most significant recalibration of the Matching Adjustment framework since Solvency II took effect in 2016. These reforms introduce new material changes that collectively alter the operational demands placed on every MA-operating firm.

We are launching a dedicated team of data engineers, quantitative analysts, and regulatory specialists focused exclusively on the data infrastructure and analytical products that MA-operating firms need to compete under the new framework. This is not a consulting overlay we leverage our proprietary algorithm utilising NLP and LLM capabilities to deliver a purpose-built capability, grounded in the technical realities of Solvency UK and designed to sit alongside your existing actuarial and investment functions.

This team exists to convert regulatory pressure into operational clarity. We build the data infrastructure and analytical tooling that risk managers, chief risk officers, and boards need to satisfy the PRA not once, but continuously, across every stage of the investment lifecycle. Solvency UK rewards firms that treat MA data infrastructure as a front-office capability. We help you build it.
