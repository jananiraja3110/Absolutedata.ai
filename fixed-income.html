Absolute Reference Data

The foundation of any sound investment strategy is accurate data. We serve as the golden source for identifiers and descriptive information, ensuring consistency across your operations.

Absolute Reference Data provides a comprehensive, accurate, and timely static data layer for a vast universe of global fixed income instruments. We present collated and verified information, including issuer details, cashflow specifics, amounts paid, option schedules (call, put, and sink provisions), and important coupon and interest payment data across Municipals, Government, Supranational, Agency, Corporate securities, and Securitised products.

Key Features

Comprehensive Instrument Coverage

Extensive data on government securities, corporate bonds, money market instruments, and more across major global markets.

Granular Data Fields

Key identifiers (ISIN, CUSIP) and hundreds of fields structural details, credit ratings from global agencies, option schedules, and more.

Rigorous Quality Assurance

Daily updates and multi-level validation checks ensuring the highest degree of data accuracy and reliability.

Integrate seamlessly via high-performance REST API designed for institutional-grade reliability and speed.
